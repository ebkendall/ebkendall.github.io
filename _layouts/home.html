Brief biography
Hello! I am happy to see you have found my website. I am a fifth year PhD candidate in the Department of Statistics at North Carolina State University (NC State). I graduated from the University of Florida (UF) with a B.S. in Mathematics and a Minor in Statistics. While at UF, I worked under the advisory of Dr. Joseph Antonelli in the Department of Statistics, and now I am advised by Dr. Jonathan Williams at NC State.
